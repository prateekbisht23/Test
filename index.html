John Cena

Wrestler, Actor, Philanthropist, Icon

Biography

John Cena is a professional wrestler, actor, and philanthropist. He is best known for his time with WWE(Chor), where he became one of the most popular and successful wrestlers in history. He has also appeared in numerous films, television shows, and has a significant presence in popular culture.

Achievements

  • 16-Time WWE World Champion
  • Member of the WWE Hall of Fame
  • Starring roles in major Hollywood films like "Fast & Furious" and "The Suicide Squad"
  • Active philanthropist and supporter of the "Make-A-Wish Foundation"
